Grading

For coding assignments, each exercise is worth some number of points. I grade both the lab and the homework together, since they are really just one assignment. I usually assign the remainder of unfinished labs as homework.

  • 50% of total points submitting code that looks like you've made some effort
  • 25% of total points for running without syntax errors
  • 25% point for producing the expected output
  • Extra credit will vary from assignment to assignment

What that means for you...

If you focus during the labs, you can get most, if not all, of your homework done!

You can get 75% of all available points by just making an effort that produces no syntax errors! At that point, I can at least help you produce the proper output.

Lastly, lab/homework assignments count for 50% of your grade
